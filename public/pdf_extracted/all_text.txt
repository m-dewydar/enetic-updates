Enetic is a power systems consultancy delivering advanced
engineering, design, and advisory services across high- and
medium-voltage networks. Our expertise spans substation
design, grid studies, testing and commissioning support, and
industrial power systems. With a strong focus on green energy
integration, microgrid solutions, and smart grid connectivity, we
support clients in building reliable, efficient, and future-ready
power infrastructure
